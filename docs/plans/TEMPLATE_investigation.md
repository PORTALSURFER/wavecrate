# Investigation Template

Use this template for focused investigations, audits, and root-cause notes.
Write down the current evidence, what it means, and what should happen next.

## Question

- What are we trying to understand?

## Evidence

- Which files, commands, or observations support the conclusion?

## Findings

- What appears to be true in the live tree?

## Recommended Next Step

- What is the smallest sensible follow-up action?

## Validation / Recheck

- How should someone confirm the findings later?
